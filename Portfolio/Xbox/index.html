Xbox 360 Sales for 2012 and Beyond!

This was the first data set that I ever did any sort of regression and time series analysis on way back in the beginning of 2012. At that point in time, the Xbox 360 was nearing the end of it lifespan (the Xbox One was showcased in May 2013) and I figured that 2012 would be the last big year that it would have for potential sales. Unfortunately, I can not locate my original report regarding my analysis (which took over 2 years as I learned new methods to handle and test out data, specifically time series data) so this will be revisited when the beer sales project is complete. I am particularly interested in trying out the fast fornier transformation on this data set.
